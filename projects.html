Credit Risk Analysis

I developed and trained a regression model as a tool for financial risk assessment, enabling accurate predictions of loan defaults and aiding institutions in risk management decisions. I demonstrated the practicality of logistic regression in financial risk assessment, emphasizing its interpretability and achieving an accuracy of 82.58% in predicting loan defaults. The implementation involved leveraging essential libraries such as NumPy, pandas, and matplotlib, and focused on key parameters like income, home ownership, employment length, loan intent, grade, and loan amount.
