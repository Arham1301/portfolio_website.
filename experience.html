Event Organizer

The 1st MIT SPEED CHESS CHAMPIONSHIP was conducted successfully on 17th & 18th November with the aim to provide a platform for chess players in the institute and attract newcomers to the game.We were thrilled to receive over 120 registrations, primarily from 1st-year students, and had about 75 participants despite scheduling conflicts with club interviews. The tournament, with no entry fees and a time control of 10 mins + 5 secs, featured 4 rounds on the first day and 3 on the second. The event ran smoothly, and the enthusiastic response encouraged us to consider organizing more such tournaments in the future.
